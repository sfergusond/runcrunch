What is RunCrunch?

RunCrunch is a free running log, performance analysis tool, and visualizer. RunCrunch was created with the goal of providing free and powerful visuals beyond the scope of typical fitness services, while keeping your fitness data easily accessible in an intuitive, interactive user interface.

RunCrunch dives deep into each activity to give you the most accurate and insightful data relevant to your athletic success, including 3D modelling, grade adjusted pace, pace zones, and intensity scoring calibrated to your fitness level.

Powered by Strava, RunCrunch is compatible with just about any fitness tracking device (i.e. Garmin, Apple Watch, Suunto, Fitbit, Peloton, and more). If you don't have a Strava account, create a free account here, sync with your fitness tracking device, and let RunCrunch handle the rest.

Advanced Analytics & Visualizations

In depth analyses of each activity includes grade adjusted pace, pace zones, and intensity scoring calibrated to your best efforts.

View an interactive pace, heartrate, and elevation graph for detailed stats on each second of your run.

Automatic and device lap breakdowns show intensity zones, time, pace, distance, and elevation statistics for each split.

3D Models

Alongside an interactive map of your run, an interactive 3D model of your activity shows pace zones, grade adjusted pace zones, and grades.

Short & Long Term Trends

Get a snapshot of your recent or long-term statistics on your dashboard. Daily statistics across a range of metrics, a unique schedule view, and a searchable activity list puts the most important information at your fingertips.

Plus, compare weekly, monthly, and yearly data for distance, time, elevation, pace, heartrate, and intensity over your lifetime.

Heatmap

Find out where you run the most, or keep track of all the locations you've run around the world in the global heatmap.
